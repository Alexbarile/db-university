ESERCIZIO 13/02/23 (query GROUP BY)

Query 1:
Contare quanti iscritti ci sono stati ogni anno

SELECT COUNT(*) AS `iscritti`, YEAR(`students`.`enrolment_date`) AS `anno`
FROM `students` 
GROUP BY YEAR(`students`.`enrolment_date`);
